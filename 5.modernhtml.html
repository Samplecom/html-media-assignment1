Semantic Elements

HTML5 introduced semantic elements like header, nav, main, section, article, aside, and footer. Utilize these elements to provide a clear and structured hierarchy to your content. Screen readers and other assistive technologies can use these elements to better understand the page's structure.

Video and Audio Accessibility

When using the video and audio elements, provide text transcripts and captions or subtitles for multimedia content. This makes videos and audio content accessible to users with hearing or visual impairments.

Semantic HTML Headings

Use proper heading tags (h1, h2, h3, etc.) to structure your content logically. Headings should form a hierarchical outline of the content, and they should not be skipped or used out of order.
